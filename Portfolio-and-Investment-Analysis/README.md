Portfolio and Investment Analysis

In this project, I used stock market data from Yahoo Finance to analyse Malibu Boats, Inc. (MBUU) and Alibaba Group Holding Limited (BABA), with the S&P 500 as a benchmark. The goal was to organise financial data in Excel and apply the portfolio and investment analysis methods studied in class.

The project includes data preparation, return calculations, descriptive statistics, and a Chi-square test for volatility. I calculated arithmetic and geometric returns, compared the behaviour of both stocks, and tested whether their annual volatility matched the 15% assumption made by a portfolio manager.

This project helped demonstrate practical quantitative and digital skills in Excel, especially in financial data analysis, statistical reasoning, and interpretation of risk.
